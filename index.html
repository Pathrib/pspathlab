Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is EasyTinda free?

EasyTinda is currently free during the early access phase. Future versions may include optional premium features.

Does EasyTinda work offline?

Yes. EasyTinda is designed to work offline for daily sales transactions. Internet connection is only required for updates and backups.

What type of business can use EasyTinda?

EasyTinda is suitable for sari-sari stores, mini marts, food stalls, and other small retail businesses.

What Android version is supported?

EasyTinda supports Android devices running Android 8.0 and above.

Is my sales data safe?

Yes. All sales data is stored locally on your device. Backup options will be available in future updates.

Is an internet connection required?

No. EasyTinda works offline. Internet is only needed for app updates and optional cloud features.

About the App

EasyTinda POS is a lightweight Android POS system designed to help small stores manage sales, products, and daily transactions efficiently. Built for speed, simplicity, and offline-friendly usage.
