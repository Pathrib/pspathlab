About the App

EasyTinda POS is a lightweight, simple, at reliable Android Point of Sale (POS) system na ginawa lalo na para sa maliliit na negosyo tulad ng sari-sari store, mini-mart, food stall, at iba pang retail businesses.

Dinisenyo ang EasyTinda POS para gawing mas mabilis, mas maayos, at mas madali ang araw-araw na bentahan. Maaari mong i-manage ang sales, products, presyo, at daily transactions nang organized at accurate kahit walang internet connection.

Hindi kailangan ng mamahaling POS machine o komplikadong setup. Kahit basic Android phone o tablet, puwede na. Dahil offline-friendly at lightweight, tuloy-tuloy ang operasyon ng negosyo kahit mahina o walang signal.

Ang EasyTinda POS ay para sa mga negosyanteng gusto ng simple pero epektibong solusyon bawas manual na sulat, bawas error, at mas malinaw na kita araw-araw.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EasyTinda free?

EasyTinda is free during early access. Premium features may be added later.

Does EasyTinda work offline?

Yes. You can record sales even without internet connection.

What businesses can use EasyTinda?

Perfect for sari-sari stores, mini marts, food stalls, and small retailers.

What Android version is supported?

Android 8.0 and above.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device.
